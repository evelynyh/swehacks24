Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a famous landmark in Paris, France. It was built in 1889 to celebrate the 100 year birthday of the French Revolution. This structure is 8,000 metallic parts and was designed by Gustave Eiffel as a temporary exhibit for the 1889 World's Fair. This tower is 330 meters tall.

You can dine with a view on the 1st floor or indulge at the Michelin-starred Le Jules Verne restaurant on the 2nd floor. At the exhilarating height of 276 meters, the top floor offers a sweeping outlook over the city of Paris and beyond. Vistas extend as far as 70 kilometers on a clear day.
